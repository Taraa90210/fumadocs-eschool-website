The **Majors** page in the Curriculum - Academic module functions to manage the list of majors available at the school.

The **Majors** page is used to record and manage all vocational programs or majors available at the school. This menu is located under the navigation:
**Curriculum → Academic → Majors**

On this page, admins can add new majors, edit existing major names, and delete majors if needed.

<img src="/images/jurusan.png" alt="Image description" />

---

### Adding a New Major

To add a new major, fill in the form on the left side of the page:

- **Name**: Enter the major name (example: TKJ, RPL, METRO)
- Click the **Save** button to save the data

Successfully added majors will appear in the **Major Registry** table on the right.

---

### Major Registry Table

The **Major Registry** table displays all active majors that have been registered. Information displayed:

- **No**: Sequence number
- **Name**: Major name
- **Actions**: Button to edit or delete data

To access the action menu, click the three-dot button in the **Actions** column.

---

### Editing a Major

<img src="/images/edit-jurusan.avif" alt="Image description" />

If you want to change a major name:

1. Click the **Edit** button in the action menu
2. The **Edit Major** modal will appear
3. Change the name as desired
4. Click **Save** to save the changes

---

### Deleting a Major

To delete a major:

1. Click the **Delete** button in the action menu
2. The major will be moved to the **Deleted** list and will no longer appear in the main list

---

### Managing Deleted Majors

Click the **Deleted** tab in the upper right of the table to view the list of majors that have been deleted.

<img src="/images/kelola-jurusan-dihapus.avif" alt="Image description" />

In this list, you have two options:

- **Restore**: Return the major to the main list
- **Permanently Delete**: Permanently remove the major data from the system
