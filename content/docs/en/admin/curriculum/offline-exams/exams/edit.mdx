### How to Edit Offline Exams in eSchool SIAKAD Plus

#### 1. Access Offline Exams Menu

- On the left sidebar, click the **"Curriculum"** menu.
- Select submenu **"Offline Exams"** > **"Exams"**.
- Here, you will see a list of registered offline exams, complete with Academic Year, Exam Name, Schedule Created, and Results Publication information.

#### 2. Select Exam to Edit

- In the **"Actions"** column on the right, click the three-dot icon (â‹®) on the exam row you want to edit.
- Several action options will appear, select **"Edit"**.

#### 3. Edit Exam Details in Modal Popup

- After selecting Edit, a modal form will appear as shown in the image.
- There are two fields that can be edited:
  - **Exam Name**: Fill in the exam name as needed.
  - **Exam Description**: Fill in with an explanation about the exam. For example:
    
    "This exam measures students' understanding of the theory and basic techniques of large ball games (soccer & basketball), including game rules, team strategies, and physical fitness benefits. The exam consists of 20 multiple choice questions with a 60-minute completion time."

- After finishing editing, click the **"Save"** button to save changes.

---

### Important Notes:

- Only users with roles that have access to the Curriculum menu can edit exams.
- Changes will be automatically saved and updated in the exam list after clicking Save.
- Ensure the inputted data is correct as this relates to student exam execution details.
