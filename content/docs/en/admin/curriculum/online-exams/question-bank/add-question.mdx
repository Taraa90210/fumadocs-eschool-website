When users press the **+Question** button on the Question Bank page, the system will open the Add Question Form page as shown in the image above. This is where teachers/admins can add new questions with various types and answer options.

---

### Add Question Form - Components & Functions

**Question Version**  
Question version (can be used for tracking question revisions).

**Question Name**  
Question name or identity (not displayed to students).

**Question Type**  
Question type options: Multiple Choice, Essay, Short Answer, True False, Numeric.

**Choice Type**  
Answer choice format: Uppercase Alphabet, Lowercase Alphabet, Numeric, Lowercase/Uppercase Roman.

**Default Points**  
Maximum score weight for this question.

**Image (Optional)**  
Upload image if the question requires visual media.

**Question Text**  
The question content that will be given to students.

**Notes (Optional)**  
Additional internal notes (not visible to students).

---

### Filling Answer Options (If Multiple Choice / True-False)

In the **Answer Options** section, users can write available answers.

Each option can be filled with:
- Answer text.
- Score percentage (e.g., 100% for correct answer).
- Feedback (explanation of why the option is correct/incorrect).

- Click **Add** to add a new answer option.
- Click **Delete** to remove unnecessary options.

---

### Available Question Types:

**Multiple Choice**  
Answers in the form of choices (A/B/C/D or others).

**Essay**  
Answers in the form of long text that must be manually graded.

**Short Answer**  
Answers in the form of one/three keywords.

**True-False**  
Questions with two answer options (True/False).

**Numeric**  
Questions with specific number answers.

---

### Choice Types (For Multiple Choice & True-False):

- **Uppercase Alphabet** → A, B, C, D
- **Lowercase Alphabet** → a, b, c, d
- **Numeric** → 1, 2, 3, 4
- **Lowercase Roman** → i, ii, iii, iv
- **Uppercase Roman** → I, II, III, IV

---

### Final Steps:

After the entire form is filled:

1. Make sure all data is correct.
2. Click the **Save** button.
3. Questions will automatically enter the Question Bank and are ready to be used in Online Exams.

---

### Functions of Add Question:

- Add new questions to the question bank according to exam needs.
- Determine score weight and question type according to curriculum.
- Adjust answer choice format to be more flexible.
