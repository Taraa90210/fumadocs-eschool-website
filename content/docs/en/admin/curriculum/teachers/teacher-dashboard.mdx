The Teacher Dashboard is a visual information center that presents current statistical data regarding teaching staff at school. This menu helps admins and school management to understand teacher composition and status quickly and efficiently.

---

### Main Purpose

Display teacher data summary in graph and number form to support strategic and operational decisions.

---

### Dashboard Components

#### **Total, Active, Inactive**

- **Total**: Total number of teachers registered in the system.
- **Active**: Teachers currently still performing their duties.
- **Inactive**: Teachers who are no longer active, either due to leave, retirement, transfer, or other reasons.

#### **Active Teacher Status**

Presented in donut chart form that visually compares the percentage of active and inactive teachers.

Example: 98.7% active teachers â€“ shows a high level of activity.

#### **Gender**

Statistics of teacher numbers by gender, presented in pie chart:
- Male
- Female

This data is useful for seeing gender representation proportions in educational institutions.

#### **Teacher Qualification**

Displayed in horizontal bar chart, including:
- PPPK (Government Employees with Work Agreements)
- PNS (Civil Servants)
- Provincial Level I Regional Honor
- School Honor Teachers
- B.Ed / S2 / Others

This visual helps to know the background and professionalism of teaching staff in aggregate.

---

### Practical Benefits

- **Quick Monitoring**: Just open the dashboard, all core info is immediately visible.
- **HR Decisions**: Easy to plan recruitment, training, or teacher distribution.
- **Proportion Evaluation**: Check gender balance or qualifications for data-based policies.

---

### Menu Access

1. Login as Admin.
2. Navigate to left sidebar > Teachers > Dashboard.
3. Data will be displayed automatically according to active semester and academic year.
