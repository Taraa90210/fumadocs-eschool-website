Manage and Monitor Teacher Attendance in Teaching

<img src="/images/guru-absen.avif" alt="Image description" />

The Teacher Attendance page in eSchool Siakad Plus is used to display and manage teacher attendance data in teaching. This feature helps the PSDM department monitor teacher attendance accurately based on class, subject, and specific date range.

---

### When to Use This Feature?

- When you want to check teacher attendance in a specific period.
- To monitor the percentage of attendance, permission, sick, and teacher absence.
- As a basis for creating teacher attendance reports.
- To make corrections or updates to student attendance status on specific meetings.

---

### Main Features

#### 1. Data Filter

<img src="/images/filter-data-guru-absen.avif" alt="Image description" />
- **Class** → Select one class or display all.
- **Start Date & End Date** → Determine the time range of the data you want to display.
- **Search** → Quick search by teacher name, subject, or class.

#### 2. Teacher Attendance Data Table

<img src="/images/tabel-absen-guru.png" alt="Image description" />

Displayed columns:
- **Teacher** → Name of the teaching teacher.
- **Subject** → Subject taught.
- **Class** → Class place of teaching.
- **Date** → Teaching time.
- **Number of Students** → Total students in that class.
- **Present, Sick, Permission, Absent** → Recap of student status in that meeting.
- **Action** → **View Detail** button to open the detailed student attendance recap.

#### 3. Data Export

<img src="/images/export-data-absen-guru.avif" alt="Image description" />
- **Export Excel** → Download report in XLSX format.
- **Export PDF** → Download report in PDF format.

#### 4. Attendance Detail

<img src="/images/detail-kehadiran-guru.avif" alt="Image description" />

Click the button in the **Action** column to display:
- Summary of the number of students per status (Present, Sick, Permission, Alpha).
- List of students along with their status.
- **Edit** button on each student to change attendance status and number of lesson hours (JP).

---

### Edit Action

<img src="/images/edit-absensi.avif" alt="Image description" />

- **Edit Attendance** → Change status (Present, Sick, Permission, Alpha) and fill in total JP.
- **Save** → Save changes.
