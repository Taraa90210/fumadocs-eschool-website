This feature is used by admins to send announcements or notifications to E-SCHOOL system users. Notifications can be addressed to all users, specific users, users with bills, or based on roles.

### Purpose

Facilitate the school in conveying important information to users in a centralized and organized manner.

---

### Menu Access

Go to the **Add Notification** page through the "Notifications" sidebar menu.

---

### Adding New Notifications

1. Click the `Add Notification` button.
2. Fill in the notification **Title** (required).
3. Fill in the notification **Message** (required).
4. (Optional) Upload an **Image** as an illustration or support.
5. Select notification recipient target:
   - **All**: Sent to all users.
   - **Specific users**: There will be additional settings after clicking `Set`.
   - **More than due cost**: Only for users with unpaid bills.
   - **Role**: Based on user role (students, teachers, etc).
6. If selecting a category other than "All", click the `Set` button to determine target user details.
7. After completion, click the `Save` button.

> *Fields with red asterisk* (`*`) are required.

---

### Deleting Notifications

1. In the **Notification List** table, find the notification you want to delete.
2. Click the **vertical three-dot icon** in the **Action** column.
3. Select the `Delete` option.
4. A confirmation pop-up will appear, select `Yes` or `Delete` to confirm.

> *There is no feature to edit notifications that have been sent. If there is an error, please delete and recreate.*

---

### Notification List Display

<img src="/images/daftar-pemberitahuan.avif" alt="Description image"/>

- **Search bar** in the upper right for quick search.
- `↻` button to *refresh* the list.
- `⬇️` button to download data (if available).

---

### Usage Tips

- Use **short and clear titles**.
- Avoid **all capital letters** except for abbreviations.
- For notifications to specific users, make sure the settings are correct in the `Set` button.

---

### Technical Notes

- The system does not support editing notifications.
- Images are optional and will be displayed as icons in the list.
- All notifications are **non-interactive**, one-way only.
