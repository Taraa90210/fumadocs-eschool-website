# Academic Year

Page for managing academic years and semester periods.

---

### Main Function

Set and manage the active academic year and the applicable semester periods in the school.

---

### Adding New Academic Year

1. Click the **Add Academic Year** button
2. Fill in the information:
   - **Name**: Example "2025/2026"
   - **Start Date**: Beginning of the academic year
   - **End Date**: End of the academic year
   - **Status**: Active/Inactive
3. Click **Save**

---

### Semester Settings

For each academic year, set the semesters:

#### Odd Semester
- Start date
- End date
- Active status

#### Even Semester
- Start date
- End date
- Active status

---

### Active Academic Year

- Only one academic year can be active at one time
- The active academic year will be used for all system modules
- Ensure dates do not overlap

---

### Academic Year List

The table displays:
- Academic year name
- Period (start date - end date)
- Status (Active/Inactive)
- Action (Edit, Delete, Activate)

---

### Usage Tips

- Create a new academic year before the current academic year ends
- Ensure all data from the previous academic year is complete before activating a new academic year
- Academic years that already have data cannot be deleted
