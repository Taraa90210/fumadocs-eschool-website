The **Help** page in **E-SCHOOL SIAKAD Plus** is a feature that makes it easier for users to get official contact information for school administrators.\
This feature serves as a communication starting point if users need technical support, feature explanations, or assistance regarding system use.

***

#### **Functions and Benefits**

1. **Quick Access to Contact Information** – Makes it easier for users to find the admin's email and phone number without having to look outside the system.
2. **Integrated Help Center** – Becomes the main reference for contacting the authorized party to provide solutions.
3. **Communication Efficiency** – Speeds up the process of conveying obstacles and problem resolution.
4. **Improving Service Support** – Ensures users always have an official communication channel with the school.

***

#### **Page Access**

* **Menu Location**:\
  Navigate to **Settings → Help** in the sidebar.
* **Information Displayed**:
  * **Admin Name** – Identity of the serving administrator.
  * **Email Address** – Official email contact for written communication.
  * **Phone Number** – Telephone contact that can be called directly for urgent needs.

***

#### **Usage Guide**

1. **Reading Contact Information**
   * Ensure that the information displayed is for an active school administrator contact.
2. **Contacting Admin via Email**
   * Click or copy the email address to send a message containing questions, obstacles, or requests for help.
3. **Contacting Admin via Phone**
   * Use the available phone number for direct communication, especially if a quick response is needed.
4. **Saving Contact**
   * It is recommended to save the admin contact information on a personal device so it can be accessed at any time.

***

#### **Important Notes**

* Use polite and clear language when contacting the admin.
* Include detailed information regarding the problem or question to facilitate the assistance process.
* Response times may vary depending on school working hours.
* Avoid contacting the admin outside of operational hours except for urgent situations.

***

If you require further assistance, contact us through the [eSchool Help page](https://www.eschool.ac.id/#contact-us).
