The **Leave Settings** menu is used to set the number of leave days per month, determine holidays, and establish leave settings based on the academic year. This feature helps the school manage work schedules and holidays in a structured manner.

***

#### **1. Add Leave Settings**

To add a new leave setting, complete the **Add Leave Settings** form consisting of:

* **Number of Leave Per Month** (*Jml Cuti Per Bulan*): Enter the number of leave days provided each month.
* **Holidays**: Select the days designated as holidays (e.g., Saturday, Sunday).
* **Academic Year**: Select the academic year applicable to these leave settings.

Click the **Save** button to add the new leave setting.

***

#### **2. Leave Settings List**

This section displays all created leave settings, with columns:

* **No**: Data sequence number.
* **Leave**: Number of leave days per month.
* **Holidays**: Days designated as holidays.
* **Academic Year**: Applicable academic year.
* **Action**: Three-dot button to edit or delete settings.

***

#### **3. Editing or Deleting Leave Settings**

1. Click the **three-dot icon** in the *Action* column of the setting row you want to modify or delete.
2. Two options will appear:
   * **Edit**: Opens the *Edit Leave* modal, where you can change the number of leave days, holidays, and academic year. After making changes, click **Save**.
   * **Delete**: Permanently removes the leave setting data. Use this option carefully as deleted data cannot be recovered.

***

#### **Benefits of This Feature**

* Ensures leave settings comply with school policy.
* Simplifies the management of holidays and annual leave.
* Optimizes planning for school activity schedules.
