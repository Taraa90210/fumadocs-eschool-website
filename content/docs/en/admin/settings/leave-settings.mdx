# Leave Settings

Page for setting policies and rules for school staff leave.

---

### Main Function

Set leave parameters and policies applicable to all school staff.

---

### Leave Policy Settings

#### Leave Types
- **Annual Leave**: Number of annual leave days per staff
- **Sick Leave**: Sick leave rules (with/without doctor's note)
- **Special Leave**: Leave for special purposes (marriage, bereavement, etc.)
- **Public Holiday**: National public holidays

#### Application Rules
- **Application Deadline**: How many days in advance to apply
- **Approval**: Required approval levels
- **Supporting Documents**: Documents that must be attached

#### Quota and Accumulation
- **Annual Quota**: Number of leave days per year
- **Carry Over**: Whether remaining leave can be carried over to the next year
- **Maximum Accumulation**: Maximum limit of leave accumulation

---

### Notification Settings

- **Application Notification**: Email to superiors when there is a new application
- **Approval Notification**: Email to staff when leave is approved/rejected
- **Reminder**: Reminder for superiors who have not processed

---

### Usage Tips

- Align policies with labor regulations
- Communicate policies clearly to all staff
- Review and update policies regularly
