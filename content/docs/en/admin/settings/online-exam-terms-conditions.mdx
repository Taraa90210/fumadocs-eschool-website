The **Online Exam Terms & Conditions** menu is used to set and display official regulations that must be followed by all online-based exam participants. This feature ensures that the exam process is conducted fairly, orderly, and in accordance with the academic integrity standards applicable at the school.

***

#### **1. Main Functions**

* Provides written guidelines that must be read and agreed upon by the exam participants before taking the online exam.
* Regulates technical and administrative provisions related to the exam implementation.
* Ensures participants understand their rights and obligations during the exam.

***

#### **2. Structure and Content**

Typically, the Online Exam Terms & Conditions document contains:

1. **Introduction**\
   Contains a statement that participants are considered to have read, understood, and agreed to all applicable terms without coercion from any party.
2. **General Participant Requirements**\
   Example:
   * Participants must be students who are registered and active at the exam organizing institution.
   * Participants are required to use official accounts provided by the institution to access the exam.
   * Participants must prepare devices (laptop/PC/tablet) with adequate specifications, a stable internet connection, and supporting applications as required (browser, Zoom, etc.).
3. **Technical Provisions** *(optional, depending on school policy)*
   * Exam schedule.
   * Duration of the exam.
   * Regulations on the use of cameras/microphones.
4. **Disciplinary Provisions and Sanctions** *(optional)*
   * Prohibition of cheating and its consequences.
   * Sanctions for violations of exam provisions.

***

***

#### **4. Benefits of This Feature**

* Ensures clarity of rules for online exam implementation.
* Reduces the risk of misunderstandings between participants and exam organizers.
* Supports a professional, transparent, and high-integrity exam process.
