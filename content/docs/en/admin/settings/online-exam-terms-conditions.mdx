Page for configuring terms and conditions that students must agree to before taking online exams.

***

### Main Functions

Manage terms and conditions content that will be displayed to students before starting online exams.

***

### Terms & Conditions Content

Configure content that includes:

#### Exam Rules

*   Exam duration
*   Number of attempts allowed
*   Rules about cheating
*   Consequences of violations

#### Technical Requirements

*   Supported browsers
*   Minimum internet connection
*   Recommended devices
*   Camera settings (if proctoring)

#### Rights and Obligations

*   Student rights during exam
*   Student obligations
*   Exam data privacy policy
*   Complaint procedures

***

### Content Editor

*   **Rich Text Editor**: Format text with heading, bold, italic, list
*   **Preview**: View display for students
*   **Versioning**: Save different versions for different exams

***

### Agreement Settings

*   **Mandatory Agreement**: Students must check agreement before starting
*   **Agreement Log**: System records time and IP of agreement
*   **Display Every Exam**: Display for every exam or once per semester

***

### Usage Tips

*   Use clear and easy-to-understand language
*   Include contact for questions
*   Update according to latest policies
*   Consult with legal team if necessary
