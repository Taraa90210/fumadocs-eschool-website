The **Payment Settings** menu is used to manage currency information and official payment methods accepted by the school. This feature ensures that all financial transactions are conducted in a structured, secure manner, and in accordance with institutional policies.

***

#### **1. Currency Settings**

This section serves to determine the type of currency and symbols used for all transactions within the system.

* **Currency**: Select the currency code (e.g., *IDR* for Indonesian Rupiah).
* **Currency Symbol**: Enter the symbol that will be displayed on transaction amounts (e.g., *Rp*).
* Click **Save** to apply the settings.

***

#### **2. Payment Methods**

This section is used to add, modify, or delete payment methods available at the school, either through bank accounts or digital wallets.

**Information that can be configured includes:**

* **Name**: The name of the bank or payment service provider (e.g., Bank Mandiri, BRI, Bank Syariah Indonesia, Dana, etc.).
* **Account Number**: A valid account number or payment account number.
* **In the Name of**: The name of the account holder or official school account.
* **Image**: The logo of the bank or provider (optional, for easier identification).

***

#### **3. Adding a New Payment Method**

1. Click the **Add** button in the *Payment Methods* section.
2. Fill in the **Name**, **Account Number**, **In the Name of** fields, and upload an image/logo if necessary.
3. Click **Save** to save the new payment method.

***

#### **4. Modifying or Deleting Payment Methods**

1. In the list of payment methods, click the **three-dot icon** in the *Action* column.
2. Two options will appear:
   * **Edit**: Opens the *Edit Payment Method* window (modal) to update data, including replacing the logo.
   * **Delete**: Permanently removes the payment method from the list. Ensure this option is only used if the method is no longer in use.

**Steps to Edit:**

* Update information as needed.
* To replace the logo, click **Upload** to select a new image file or **Remove Image** to get rid of the existing logo.
* Click **Save** to save the changes.

***

#### **Benefits of This Feature**

* Simplifies centralized management of school account data.
* Avoids payment information errors for students or parents/guardians.
* Enhances transparency and accuracy in school financial transactions.
