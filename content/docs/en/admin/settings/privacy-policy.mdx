Page for setting a privacy policy that explains how user data is collected, used, and protected.

---

### Main Function

Manage transparent privacy policy content about data management practices in the eSchool system.

---

### Privacy Policy Content

#### Information Collected
- **Personal Data**: Name, address, email, phone number
- **Academic Data**: Grades, attendance, tasks
- **Financial Data**: Payment history
- **Technical Data**: Access logs, IP address, cookies

#### How Data is Collected
- Account registration
- System use
- Cookies and tracking technology
- Third-party integration

#### Data Use
- Provision of educational services
- Communication with users
- Service improvement
- Legal compliance

#### Data Security
- Data encryption
- Limited access
- Regular backups
- Security audit

---

### User Rights

- **Access**: See stored personal data
- **Correction**: Fix inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request data deletion (by provision)
- **Portability**: Get a copy of data
- **Objection**: Refuse certain data processing

---

### Data Sharing

Explain when and with whom data is shared:
- Third parties (payment gateway, email service)
- Educational authorities
- Law enforcement (if needed)

---

### Content Editor

- **Rich Text Editor**: Professional format
- **Legal Review**: Mark for legal review
- **Effective Date**: Effective date
- **Change Log**: Change history

---

### Usage Tips

- Align with data protection regulations (GDPR, UU PDP)
- Use easy-to-understand language
- Update when there are changes in data practices
- Consult with a privacy legal expert
