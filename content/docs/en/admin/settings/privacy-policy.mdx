The **Privacy Policy** page serves to provide official information to users regarding how **E-SCHOOL SIAKAD Plus** collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal data they provide while using the service.\
This policy is a form of the platform's commitment to maintaining information confidentiality and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

***

#### **Functions and Benefits**

1. **Data Management Transparency** – Explains to users how their personal data is used.
2. **User Rights Protection** – Ensures users understand their rights regarding personal data.
3. **Regulatory Compliance** – Follows data protection provisions in accordance with applicable laws.
4. **User Trust** – Provides a sense of security when users access and use the service.

***

#### **Page Access**

* **Menu Location**:\
  Navigate to **Settings → Privacy Policy** in the sidebar.
* **Public URL**:\
  Displayed at the top of the page so it can be shared with relevant parties such as students, teachers, or parents/guardians.
* **Content Editor**:\
  Uses a *Rich Text Editor* to facilitate writing, formatting, and updating policy content.

***

#### **Usage Guide**

1. **Reading the Policy**
   * Ensure the entire content of the policy complies with the school's internal policies and legal regulations.
2. **Editing Content**
   * Use the editor to add, delete, or update information according to evolving needs and regulations.
3. **Saving Changes**
   * Click the **Save** button to apply updates and display them on the public URL.
4. **Coordination with Relevant Parties**
   * Consult policy updates with school management before publishing.

***

#### **Important Notes**

* Use clear, concise, and easy-to-understand language for all users.
* Perform regular updates, especially if there are changes in regulations or school policies.
* Ensure the published policy is aligned with the **Terms & Conditions** to avoid contradictions.
* Inform all users officially of major changes.
