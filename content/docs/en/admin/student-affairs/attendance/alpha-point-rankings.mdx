The **Alpha Point Rankings** feature in eSchool is an important tool in monitoring and evaluating student attendance levels based on the **number of alpha hours (absent without explanation)** that have accumulated. This page automatically compiles **rankings of students with the most alpha points**, both for all levels and per grade level.

### Feature Purpose

* Provides a quick overview of students with the highest absence levels.
* Helps the student affairs team and homeroom teachers in **identifying students who need special attention**.
* Becomes the basis for **further coaching or counseling follow-ups**.
