The Student Dashboard is a visual information center that presents statistics and overall data related to students in real-time at eSchool. This menu is designed so that admins, principals, and school operators can obtain a comprehensive overview of student conditions and composition quickly and easily understood.

---

### Main Information

The top of the dashboard displays four key indicators:

| Indicator | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Number of Students** | Total overall students registered in the system. |
| **Active Students** | Number of students currently with active status. |
| **Inactive Students** | Number of students who have left, graduated, or other inactive. |
| **Number of Guardians** | Total guardian accounts registered and connected with students. |

---

### Statistical Visualization

#### **Active Students**
- Donut chart comparing the proportion of active and inactive students.
- Example: 95.9% active and 4.1% inactive.

#### **Gender**
- Displays the ratio of Male vs Female from all students.
- Example: 65.6% male, 34.4% female.

#### **Grade Level**
Percentage distribution of students by level:
- Grade X
- Grade XI
- Grade XII

Useful for monitoring the balance of student numbers at each level.

#### **Major**
Statistics of student numbers by major:
- RPL, TKJ, ELIN, METRO

Shows the distribution of interests and potential class/major ratios.

---

### Filters and Interaction

- A class filter dropdown is available in the upper right (default: "All Classes") that allows users to adjust the displayed data based on specific levels.
- Data will be automatically updated according to the selected filter.

---

### Main Benefits

- **Quick Monitoring**: Provides a visual and informative summary of student data.
- **Decision Making**: Helps school management in formulating academic strategies and policies based on factual data.
- **Imbalance Detection**: With gender, major, and level visualization, schools can anticipate imbalances from the start.

---

### Notes

- This data is aggregate, not per individual. For detailed student data, use the **Students** > **Student List** menu.
- Make sure student data is always updated so that visualization remains accurate.
