# Payroll

Page for managing and monitoring teacher and staff salary payments.

---

### 1. Add Salary

At the top of the page there is an **Add Salary** form that functions to display salary data based on a specific period. Users can select:
- **Month**: Determine the payroll month to display.
- **Year**: Determine the relevant payroll year.

After selecting month and year, click the **Search** button to load salary data according to that period.

---

### 2. Salary List

The main part of the page displays the **Salary List** table, containing detailed data for each teacher or staff. Available columns include:
- **No**: Data sequence number.
- **Name**: Teacher or staff name.
- **Base Salary (Rp)**: Base salary received before additions or deductions.
- **Allowances (Rp)**: Additional income such as position or transportation allowances.
- **Deductions (Rp)**: Salary deductions such as tardiness or contributions.
- **Net Salary (Rp)**: Final amount after allowances are added and deductions are subtracted.
- **Status**: Shows whether salary has been paid or not.

#### Filter and Settings
- **Payment Date**: Determines the salary payment recording date.
- **Status**: Can be filtered to display all data, only paid, or unpaid.
- Page navigation and settings for the number of data rows displayed per page are also available.

---

### 3. Payment Status

Each data row has a **Status** column with two possibilities:
- **Paid**: Indicates salary has been paid.
- **Unpaid**: Indicates salary is still awaiting payment process.

If the status is **Paid**, the **Action** button on the right side of the table will be active. Users can click that button to access the salary slip which contains detailed payment details for teachers or staff.

If the status is still **Unpaid**, the Action button will be inactive (disabled) until payment is made.

---

### 4. Save

After making changes or filling in salary data, users can press the **Save** button at the bottom of the page to save all changes made.
