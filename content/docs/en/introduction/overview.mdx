# **Welcome**
An Integrated School Management Platform for a Better Future of Education

## **eSchool Overview**
eSchool is an integrated school management platform designed to simplify learning management, academic data, communication, and school operations in one integrated solution. With an intuitive interface and comprehensive features, eSchool helps educational institutions to digitally transform easily and efficiently.



## **Features and Modules**
Discover various complete modules that will optimize your school management

### **Messages**
Real-time communication between teachers, students, parents & staff.

### **Student Management**
Complete student data: biodata, attendance, achievements, academics.

### **Forms**
Input & collect data from various school activities.

### **Announcements**
Important information directly to all or groups of users.

### **Contacts & Directory**
Manage all internal school contact information.

### **User Accounts**
Manage access rights & accounts based on user roles.

### **Teacher Module**
Input grades, give assignments, and monitor classes easily.

### **Parent Module**
View grades, schedules, attendance, and school announcements.

### **School Subscription**
Admin manages service status & active features.

### **Payments**
Manage bills, payments, and transaction receipts.

### **System Settings**
User & school profiles, files, custom labels, and module access.
