We value your privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

---

### Data Collected

#### Personal Data
- **Identity**: Full name, date of birth, gender
- **Contact**: Email, phone number, address
- **Documents**: Photos, ID Card/Family Card (for verification)

#### Academic Data
- **Grades**: Exam results, assignments, and assessments
- **Attendance**: Student and teacher attendance records
- **Activity**: Activity logs within the system

#### Financial Data
- **Payments**: Transaction history and bills
- **Payment Method**: Account information (encrypted)

#### Technical Data
- **Access Logs**: IP address, browser, access time
- **Cookies**: User preferences and login sessions

---

### Use of Data

The collected data is used for:

1. **School Operations**
   - Student and teacher management
   - learning and assessment process
   - Financial administration

2. **Communication**
   - School announcements
   - Important notifications
   - Newsletters and updates

3. **Service Improvement**
   - System usage analysis
   - Feature and performance improvements
   - Personalization of user experience

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Meeting regulatory obligations
   - Reporting to education authorities
   - Audits and investigations

---

### Data Sharing

#### Parties Who Can Access Data

**Internal**:
- School admins (full access)
- Teachers (student data in their classes)
- Students (their own personal data)
- Parents (their children's data)

**External** (with consent):
- **Payment Gateway**: For payment processing
- **Email Service**: For sending notifications
- **Cloud Storage**: For data backup
- **Education Authorities**: For mandatory reporting

**Not Shared**:
- We DO NOT sell your data to third parties
- We DO NOT use data for advertising
- We DO NOT share data without your consent

---

### User Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View personal data we store
2. **Correction**: Fix inaccurate data
3. **Deletion**: Request data deletion (according to provisions)
4. **Portability**: Get a copy of your data
5. **Objection**: Refuse certain data processing
6. **Restriction**: Limit the use of your data

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@eschool.ac.id

---

### Data Security

We protect your data with:
- End-to-end encryption
- Limited role-based access
- Regular and secure backups
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Periodic security audits

See the [Security](./security) page for more details.

---

### Data Retention

- **Active Data**: During the active period as a user
- **Archive Data**: 5 years after graduation/resignation (according to regulations)
- **Financial Data**: 10 years (according to tax regulations)
- **System Logs**: 1 year

---

### Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:
- Maintain your login session
- Remember your preferences
- Usage analysis (anonymized)

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.
