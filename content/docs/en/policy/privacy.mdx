### Our Commitment to Digital Privacy in Education

#### üìå Our Privacy Commitment

At eSchool, we believe that **privacy is a fundamental right**, especially in an educational environment. Therefore, we are committed to:

* Only collecting data that is **truly necessary** for academic operations and school administration.
* **Not selling, sharing, or monetizing user personal data** ‚Äî including in the free version of our services.
* Communicating this policy information in **clear, transparent, and easy-to-understand** language.

This policy aims to openly explain:

* The types of data we collect
* How data is used and stored
* Your rights to data control and management

***

#### üåê Scope of the Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all digital services within the eSchool ecosystem, including:

* **eSchool Mobile App** (Android & iOS)
* **Admin Dashboard** and **eSchool SIAKAD Web**
* **Official third-party APIs and system integrations**
* Interactions through forms, help services, and legitimate digital communication

> ‚ùó This policy **does not apply** to external services or third parties that have separate privacy policies (e.g., video conferencing, payment gateways, etc.).

***

#### üìÇ Privacy Policy Structure

**I. Information Collected and Controlled by eSchool**

Data is collected when:

* School account registration
* Users log in or access services
* Filling out forms or contacting the help team

Types of data include:

* Name, email, Student/Staff ID (NIS/NIP), user role
* Class information, subjects, schedule, and academic records
* Technical data: IP address, device type, activity/login history

Use of data:

* Providing teaching-learning services and school management
* User verification & maintaining system security
* Providing assistance according to user needs

***

**II. Information Processed on Behalf of the School**

As an information system provider, eSchool **processes data based on school instructions**.\
We will not use student, teacher, or parent data for our internal purposes **unless there is written consent from the institution owning the data.**

***

**III. General Provisions**

* We maintain data security in accordance with the standards in the eSchool Security Policy.
* Any changes to this policy will be notified through the app or email.
* Users can request updates or deletion of personal data through the school admin.
