**eSchool SIAKAD Plus** is a school management platform based on *Software as a Service (SaaS)* specifically designed for the digitalization of all academic, administrative, and school communication activities. Used by thousands of educational institutions in Indonesia, data security is one of the main principles in every development and improvement of our services.

***

#### üè¢ Organizational Security

We implement an **Information Security Management System (ISMS)** that aligns with the best standards in the educational technology industry. This system includes policies, procedures, and technical controls to protect data from all forms of threats.

Basic principles we apply at eSchool SIAKAD Plus:

* **Data Confidentiality**\
  User data, including personal data of students, teachers, and parents, is strictly guarded and will not be shared with third parties without explicit permission.
* **Data Integrity**\
  Every academic and administrative data is ensured to be accurate, complete, and free from unauthorized manipulation.
* **System Availability**\
  The system is designed with high-availability architecture, with automatic backups and high uptime, so that schools can access services whenever needed.
