The **Class Schedule** page in the **eSchool** platform provides an interactive and easy-to-read weekly visual overview, tailored specifically for each teacher. Through this feature, teachers can view and manage their teaching hours in a concise and informative **daily calendar** format.

<img src="/images/jadwal-pelajaran.avif" alt="Image description" />

### **Purpose of this Feature**

*   Display the weekly distribution of a teacher's instruction hours.
*   Simplify the monitoring of active class schedules without needing to refer to separate documents.
*   Provide rapid access to the specific times and classes assigned to the teacher.

---

### **View and Navigation**

#### **Teacher Identity**
The teacher's full name is automatically displayed at the top of the page, indicating that the schedule is personalized.
