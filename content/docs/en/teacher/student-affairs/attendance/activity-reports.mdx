The **Activity Attendance Report** page in eSchool is designed to record and monitor student attendance in **non-lesson activities.** This feature separates special activity attendance from routine daily and per-subject attendance, so that reporting becomes more specific and orderly.

<img src="/images/laporan-absensi-kegiatan-khusus.png" alt="Image description"  />

## Navigation and Filters

#### Data Filter

Users can filter reports based on:

* **Class**: Displays special activity reports per class.
* **Start Date & End Date**: Specifies the time range of activity recording.
* **Attendance Type**: Filters data based on attendance status (Present, Sick, Permission, Alpha).

This filter facilitates presency data search for certain periods and groups.

### Additional Features

* **Search Bar**: Facilitates quick student name search.
* **Export Data**: Buttons are available to download reports in Excel or PDF format.
* **Pagination**: Navigation between pages to monitor more than 10 entries per page.

***

### Benefits of This Feature

| User | Specific Benefit |
| :--- | :--- |
| Activity Teachers | Recording active activity participants outside class precisely |
| Homeroom Teachers | Knowing student activeness in non-academic programs |
| Student Affairs | Creating discipline & student activeness reports across school activities |
| Management | Getting student attendance insights in the context of institutional activities |

***

### Tips Usage

* Use the **date** filter for activity recaps per event.
* Download PDF reports for school documentation archives.
* Monitor students who are often absent in school activities for counseling/student affairs intervention.
