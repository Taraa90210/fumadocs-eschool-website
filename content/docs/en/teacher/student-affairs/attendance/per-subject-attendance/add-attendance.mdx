The **Add Attendance** feature allows teachers to record student attendance in real-time for each specific subject session. This form must be filled in correctly so that attendance data is recorded neatly and can be used in official school reports.

<img src="/images/tambah-absensi.avif" alt="Image description" className="mx-auto block max-w-full" />

### Steps to Fill in the Form

#### 1. **Select Class**

* Select the class where the attendance is conducted from the dropdown.
* Example: `XI TKJ C`.

***

#### 2. **Select Lesson Schedule**

* Select the subject session from the dropdown based on active lesson hours.
* Example: `MULOK PJOK : 07:02 - 14:59`.

***

#### 3. **Fill in Attendance Date**

* Select the attendance date in the calendar format.
* Example: `30-07-2025`.

***

#### 4. **Write Material (Optional)**

* If needed, fill in the topic or material taught at that meeting.
* Example: "Introduction to PJOK Basics".

***

#### 6. **Use "All Present" Feature (Optional)**

* If all students are present, click the **"All Present"** button to automatically fill in all student statuses as **Present**.
* You can still change individual statuses afterwards if needed.

***
