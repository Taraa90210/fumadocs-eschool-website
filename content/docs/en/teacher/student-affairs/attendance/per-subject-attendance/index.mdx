The **Per-Subject Attendance** page in the eSchool system is an important feature that allows teachers and admins to record and review student attendance based on the **subjects** taking place on a **specific day**.

<img src="/images/absensi-per-pelajaran.avif" alt="Image description" className="mx-auto block max-w-full" />

### Main Functions

* Record attendance for each subject taught.
* Display student attendance recapitulation based on lesson hours.
* Help teachers in class monitoring and teaching hour reports.

### How to Use the Per-Subject Attendance Page

#### 1. **Select Class and Date Range**

* Use the **Class** dropdown to specify the class you want to review.
* Set the **Start Date** and **End Date** to filter attendance data for a specific time range.
* Example: `XI TKJ C`, `30-07-2025 to 30-07-2025`.

***

#### 2. **View Displayed Attendance Data**

The table will display information such as:

* **Subject**: Name of the lesson, e.g., "MULOK PJOK"
* **Date**: Time the attendance was conducted
* **Number of Students**
* **Attendance Status**:
  * Present: Students who followed the lesson
  * Sick: Students absent due to illness
  * Permission: Students who did not attend with official permission
  * Absent: Alpha/without explanation
