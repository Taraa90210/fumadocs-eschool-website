Full guide to reset your password if you forget it.

### **Password Reset Procedure**

If you have forgotten your password, follow these steps to securely reset it and regain access to your account:

1.  **Navigate to the Login Page**: Open the eSchool login screen.
2.  **Click "Forgot Password?"**: Look for this link typically located below the login credentials fields.
3.  **Enter Your Registered Email**: Provide the email address associated with your eSchool account.
4.  **Check Your Email Inbox**: You will receive a password reset link from eSchool.
5.  **Click the Reset Link**: Follow the instructions provided in the email.
6.  **Create a New Password**: Enter a strong, unique password and confirm it.
7.  **Log In**: Use your new credentials to access your account.

---

### **Security Tips**

- **Use a Strong Password**: Include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Keep it Confidential**: Never share your login credentials with anyone.
- **Update Regularly**: Consider changing your password periodically for enhanced security.
