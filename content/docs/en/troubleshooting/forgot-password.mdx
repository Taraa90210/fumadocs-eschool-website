The **Forgot Password** feature in eSchool SIAKAD Plus allows users who have forgotten their passwords to perform account resets independently. This process is fast, secure, and only requires a school code and an active registered email.

***

### üßæ When to Use This Feature?

* When a user cannot log in because they have forgotten their password.
* When wanting to reset the password for security reasons.

***

### üõ†Ô∏è Password Reset Guide

1. **Open the eSchool Website**\
   Open the eSchool website by accessing the link [https://eschool.ac.id/](https://eschool.ac.id/)\
   Click the **"Login"** button at the top.

   <img src="/images/login-reset.avif" alt="eSchool Logo" />

***

2. **Access the Login Page**\
   Click the **"Forgot Password?"** button below the login fields.

   <img src="/images/lupa-password.avif" alt="eSchool Logo" />

***

3. **Enter Reset Information**\
   Fill out the form with the following data:
   * **School Code**: Enter the school code, e.g., `SCH20247`
   * **Email**: Use the active email address that has been registered in the system.

   <img src="/images/send-reset.avif" alt="eSchool Logo" />

***

4. **Send Reset Link**\
   Click the **Send Password Reset Link** button.\
   The system will send a link to change the password via the entered email.

***

5. **Check Your Email**\
   Open your email and click the provided link to set a new password.

***

### üí° Additional Tips

* Ensure the email used matches the one registered in the system.
* Do not share the password reset link with anyone else to maintain account security.
* If you do not receive the reset email, check your spam/junk folder.
* If it still doesn't work, contact the school administrator or the eSchool Help Team.

***

If you require further assistance, contact us through the [eSchool Help page](https://www.eschool.ac.id/#contact-us).
