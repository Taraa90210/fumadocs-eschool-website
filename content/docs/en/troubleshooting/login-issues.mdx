If you experience problems when logging in to eSchool, please follow the troubleshooting guide below.

### **Common Solutions**

If you encounter difficulties while attempting to sign into your eSchool account, please review the following troubleshooting steps:

1.  **Check Credentials**: Double-check your username and password for any typos. Ensure Caps Lock is off.
2.  **Clear Browser Cache**: Sometimes outdated cached data can cause login failures. Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
3.  **Stable Internet Connection**: Ensure you have a reliable internet connection.
4.  **Supported Browser**: Use a modern, supported web browser like Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox.
5.  **Contact School Admin**: If the problem persists, your account may be locked or inactive. Reach out to your school's IT or administrative department.

---

### **Account Security**

- **Unauthorized Access**: If you suspect your account has been compromised, reset your password immediately and notify the administrator.
- **Log Out**: Always log out of your session on public or shared computers.
