# Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it using the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page or by contacting your administrator.

## Reset Steps

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click on **Forgot Password?**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Follow the instructions sent to your email.
